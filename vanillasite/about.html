What you should know

For most of us, our home is our biggest asset. As a homeowner myself, I understand that things come up, and home services can be expensive. In some cases, it might be worth the risk to save some money. Lawn mowing is a good example in this case. If the contractor does a half hearted job, you might end up with an uneven lawn and a dirty driveway. The good news is all you need to do is sweep your driveway wait a week or two for your lawn to grow back. Pressure washing, however, is different. Using too much pressure can damage and void warranties on roofs, siding of all different types, and concrete. Plants can be damaged, paint can be bleached (picture a bleach stained front door), and electrical outlets can be damaged. There are times when saving money can be wise. Pressure washing your house is not one of those times. Hire the best and rest easy knowing that we will treat your property like it was our own.
